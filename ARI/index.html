Data Dashboards

  • Alcohol-related Crime [I]

    Interactive Static Mapping

    This interactive map shows the spatial distirbution of alcohol related crime in Arlington County from 2015 to 2018. The dashboard also provide temporal analysis filter for users to explore the distribution of crime by year, month, and time of the day

  • Alcohol-Related Crime [II]

    Interactive Dynamic Mapping

    This dynamic maps visualize the spatial distirbution of alcohol-related crime by month and by crime type!

  • Social Impact Analysis Dashboard

    Interactive Static Mapping

    This datadashboard shows the [selected] collected measures (demographic, socioeconomic, crime, and perception indices) at the census block group level

  • Economic Impact Analysis Dashboard

    Interactive Static Mapping

    This datadashboard shows the [selected] collected measures (property values per sqft, restaurant employment, etc.) at the census block group level
